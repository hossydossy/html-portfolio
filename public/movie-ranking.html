The Best Movies According to Paul

My top 3 movies of all time

Bladerunner

An absolute classic Philip K. Dick adaptation from Ridley Scott. Sheer beauty.

The Commitments

Roddy Doyle's masterpiece brought to life and a soundtrack that will break your heart.

La Haine

A black-and-white classic set around the Parisian race riots. Visceral, emotional, raw and funny.
